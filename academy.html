Why choose us?
When it comes to building a successful career in makeup artistry, who you learn from matters just as much as what you learn.
At FrimoussE, you're not just taught makeup — you're mentored by two icons who have lived and led in the real world of beauty, cinema, and bridal artistry.
‍
Fizza Ali, the Most Awarded Makeup Artist in South India, and Ester Noronha, an acclaimed actress and style icon, bring you a unique blend of technical mastery, artistic intuition, and hands-on industry knowledge.
‍
In today’s competitive world, it’s not enough to know theory. You need to master the art of dealing with real brides, celebrities, and diverse faces — each with their own challenges like skin textures, pigmentation, undertones, and unique expectations. That’s exactly the kind of real-world readiness we prepare you for.
What Sets Us Apart:
Direct Learning from Fizza Ali and Ester Noronha
No substitutes. You learn directly from two celebrated names who have perfected their craft on real brides, celebrities, and global stages.
Signature Celebrity Techniques
Learn the exclusive techniques behind red-carpet glam, bridal glow-ups, and flawless editorial makeup — skills you won’t find in generic textbooks.
Real Client & Bride-Focused Training
From the challenges of humid outdoor weddings to high-definition cameras, our course trains you to create looks that are beautiful, long-lasting, and photography-ready.
Bridal, Editorial, and Red Carpet Focus
Our curriculum blends the best of bridal, fashion, and celebrity makeup artistry so you’re career-ready across industries.
Hands-On Practice
You’ll work with diverse models to sharpen your skill, confidence, and speed under real-time conditions.
Soft Skills & Client Management
Learn how to consult, communicate, and manage real clients with professionalism — an essential part of being a top artist.
Certificate of Completion
Boost your portfolio and credibility with certification personally signed by Fizza Ali and Ester Noronha.
