Gloss, Glitz & Glam! Know Our Opulent Duo!
At FrimoussE, we don’t follow trends—we craft them. Co-founded by Ester Valerie Noronha, an award-winning actress & skincare expert-turned-beauty entrepreneur, and Fizza Ali, our award-winning elite beauty & skincare expert expert who is well-known in the industry for her game-changing transformations. With well-over 3000 clients, FrimoussE is where glam meets greatness.
Meet Ester Valerie Noronha – The Star Who Shines Beyond the Screen & Fizza Ali – The Magic Behind the Glitz
From dazzling on the silver screen to redefining beauty, Ester Valerie Noronha is not your average salon owner. Ester's an award-winning actress, singer, and performer, and has captivated audiences in seven languages. Having ruled the entertainment industry for over a decade, she knows how to make heads turn - on and off camera. You might think, why a salon? Well that's simple - Ester created this haven believing that beauty belongs to all. Her vision of providing a space where everyone can Slay The Day! Whether it’s a red carpet event or a brunch with your BFF, she’s got you covered with the ultimate glam squad at FrimoussE!
You know those flawless celebrity looks that make you wonder, "How do they even do that?!" Well, meet Fizza Ali, the artist behind the brush. With well over 13 years of experience, a trailblazing career in the beauty industry, and a portfolio jam-packed with A-list celebrities. Fizza is the go-to makeup artist for South India’s elite. But she’s not just about creating stunning makeovers - she’s on a quest to nurture the next generation of makeup artists. Whether you’re a bride, an aspiring MUA, or you just want to slay every day, Fizza’s got the Glam Touch! Her haven at FrimoussE isn’t just about learning the craft, it’s about mastering confidence, style, and the art of making heads turn!
Ready to get glammed up? Schedule a visit, enroll in a class, or just stop by to experience the magic at FrimoussE - where artistry blends with elegance, and every makeover slays!
